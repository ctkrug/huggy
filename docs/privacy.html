Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

Huggy ("the App") is a private couples app. We collect only the minimum data needed to deliver hugs between you and your partner. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

What We Collect

  • Account information — Your Apple ID or email address, used solely for authentication via Firebase Authentication.
  • Display name — The name you provide or that comes from your Apple ID, shown to your partner inside the app.
  • Partner link — When you pair with your partner, we store both user IDs together so hugs can be delivered between you.
  • Hug data — The type of hug sent, optional notes (max 60 characters), and timestamps. This is visible only to you and your partner.
  • Push notification token — A device token provided by Apple, used to deliver hug notifications. This token does not identify you personally.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your identity
  • Pair you with your partner
  • Send and receive hugs between you and your partner
  • Deliver push notifications when you receive a hug
  • Track your hug streak

Data Storage

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for sign-in
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore — for data storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — for push notifications

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your hug data is visible only to you and your paired partner.

Data Deletion

You can unpair from your partner at any time in Settings. To delete your account entirely, contact us and we will remove all your data from our systems within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Huggy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
