Huggy is a private little app just for you and your partner. Send virtual hugs anytime you're thinking of them, request a real one when you need it most, and watch your hug streak grow day by day.

Choose from 10 unique hug types — from a warm Bear Hug to a gentle Butterfly Hug — each with its own animated character. Unlock special hugs by keeping your streak alive.

Because sometimes you just need to feel close, even when you're apart.
